🏆 Established 1985

40 Years of Dressing Bardoli's Kids

AAVKAR Collection started in 1985 with one simple mission — to give every child in Bardoli access to beautiful, comfortable, and affordable clothing. Over four decades, we've become Bardoli's most trusted children's fashion store.

From a newborn's very first outfit to a teenager's festival look — we've been part of every milestone for thousands of families across Bardoli and South Gujarat.
